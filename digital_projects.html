Chinese Agricultural Scientists

The Chinese Agricultural Scientist Database (1900-1949) is a prosopographical database that aims to transcribe and analyze personnel and employment records for Chinese agricultural scientists from the first half of the twentieth century.

Republican China Corpus

The Republican China Periodicals Corpus represents a collaboration between the University of Chicago and the Chinese Periodical Database at the Shanghai Library. It contains 72 cultural and literary periodicals (full run) from 1918-1949.
