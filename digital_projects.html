Chinese Agricultural Scientists

The Chinese Agricultural Scientist Database (1900-1949) is a prosopographical database that aims to transcribe and analyze personnel and employment records for Chinese agricultural scientists from the first half of the twentieth century.

Republican China Corpus

The Republican China Periodicals Corpus represents a collaboration between the University of Chicago and the Chinese Periodical Database at the Shanghai Library. It contains 72 cultural and literary periodicals (full run) from 1918-1949.

Chinese Association of Agricultural Science Database

The Chinese Association of Agricultural Science is the oldest agricultural science organization in China established in 1917. From 1918-1948, the association published at least 190 issues of The Journal of Agriculture and Forestry, including over 2,000 individual scientific articles. As the most important journal for agricultural science in Republican China (1912-1949), it offers insights into the growth and evolution of science in China. The first step of this project will focus on producing a text version of the journal using ABBYY Cloud OCR SDK. Future work will include compiling metadata for the scientists published in the pages of the journal.
